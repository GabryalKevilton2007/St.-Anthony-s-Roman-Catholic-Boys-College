Welcome to the Primary Section

The primary years at St. Anthony’s RCBC are the most crucial. Here, we focus on literacy, numeracy, and character building. We ensure that every boy feels safe, valued, and encouraged to explore his talents.

Our Core Curriculum

Mathematics

Focusing on logic and mental math.

Languages

Sinhala, English, and Tamil literacy.

Religious Studies

Spiritual growth and moral values.

Creative Arts

Music, Art, and Drama sessions.

  • Junior Athletics
  • Little Friends Debating
  • Chess Club
